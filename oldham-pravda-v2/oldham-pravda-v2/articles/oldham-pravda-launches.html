Oldham Pravda launches online

By Oldham Pravda

Oldham Pravda now has a permanent online home for local news, opinion, council scrutiny and community reporting.

Social media is useful, but posts disappear quickly. This website is designed to become an archive: a place where stories can be found, shared and kept.

This is the first version. More sections, more articles and more tools will be added as the site grows.
